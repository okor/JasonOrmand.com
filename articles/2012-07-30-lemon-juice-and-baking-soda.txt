I'm interested in macro videography so I gave it a shot.

I used a Panasonic GH2, a kit 14-42mm lens and some cheap bellows. The solution is in a whiskey glass sitting on top of an iPhone with a full screen white image, max brightness, never sleep and a piece of plain white paper to diffuse.
